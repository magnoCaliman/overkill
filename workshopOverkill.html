1 FUNCTIONS & PARAMETERS

1.1 'ONESHOT' FUNCTIONS

  • play n
    • ops: attack / release
  • sleep n
  • sample :foo
    • ops: rate 
sample :ambi_glass_hum
play 64, release: 1.8
sleep 0.1
play 63, release: 2
play 62, release: 3
sleep 2
sample :ambi_haunted_hum, rate: 0.5
play 90, attack: 4, release: 6
sleep 2
play 88, attack: 4, release: 4
  • size()
  • point()
  • line()
  • rect()
  • ellipse()

1.2 GLOBAL BEHAVIOUR CHANGING FUNCTIONS

  • usesynthdefaults 
use_synth :fm

use_synth :dsaw
use_synth_defaults release: 5, detune: 0.2

play 67
play 70
play 74

sleep 5

use_synth :prophet
use_synth_defaults release: 0.2

play 67
sleep 0.2
play 70
sleep 0.2
play 74
